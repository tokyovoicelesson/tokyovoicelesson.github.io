About Me

Hello everyone.

I've been a voice trainer in Tokyo for 10 years.

I have taught students how to sing J-POP and ANIME songs at entertainment agencies and music schools.

Recently, I’m also active as a singing director for recording artists.

As a music creator, I provide music to many artists.

The songs have been Sung on TV and have been used as theme songs and insert songs for TV dramas and ANIME. I won the Japan Record Awards.

We started this project so that I can return my experience to J-POP fans all over the world.

I have taught students from 7 to 60 years old, so please feel free to take lessons even if you are a beginner!

Basically, the lessons are conducted in Japanese, but a little English can be used.
