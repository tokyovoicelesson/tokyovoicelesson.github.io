About Me

Hello everyone.

I've been a voice trainer in Tokyo for 10 years.

I have taught how to sing J-POP and ANIME songs at entertainment agencies and music schools.

Recently, I also active as a singing director in recording.

As a music creator, I provide music to many artists. 

The songs have been sung on TV and became the theme song and insert song for TV dramas and ANIME. I won the Japan Record Awards.  

We started this project so that I can return my experience to J-POP fans all over the world.

I have taught  from 15 to 60 years old, so please feel free to take lessons even if you are beginner!

Basically, the lessons are conducted in Japanese, but a little English can be used.
